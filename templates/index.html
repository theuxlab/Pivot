About Morneau Shepell

Morneau Shepell is one of the world’s largest Employee Assistance Programme (EAP) providers, supporting employees and their families across the globe to resolve work, health, and life challenges. We serve more than 20,000 global organizations, supporting over 20 million individuals to improve their lives.

Since 1966, we have formed trusted, long-term partnerships with our clients, helping them improve organizations effectiveness while having a positive impact in the lives of their people.
